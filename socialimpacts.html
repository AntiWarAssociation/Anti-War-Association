The Social Impacts of War



War can destroy entire families and communities,

as well as cause (negative) lasting psychological effects to some people,

especially children.

War's Impact on Minority Groups



In the case of Indigenous peoples, those who have a strong connection to or live

in harmony with the environment would be greatly

impacted by the destruction caused by war. An example of this occurring is during the Vietnam war

when the Montagnards (Indigenous people of the Central Highlands of Vietnam)

were driven off their land and persecuted by the communist government.
