War - What is it?



A war is a period of conflict and hostility

involving two or more parties usually due

to an ideological, territorial, religious

or political dispute.

Evolution of Warfare



War has posed a greater threat to humanity as the scale of wars increased over time.

What began as throwing and bludgeoning of clubs and spears between tribes grew to the swinging

of swords and the firing of arrows between civilisations, eventually reaching the exchange of gunfire and the

dropping of bombs between nations. Where humanity is today, another worldwide conflict

could be the end of our species, the threat of nuclear weaponry is far too great,

and cyberwarfare is on the rise.
